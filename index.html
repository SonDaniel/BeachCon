FAQ

Registration

  • Check-in starts promptly at NOON on Saturday and 10 a.m. on Sunday. NO ONE is allowed to enter the venue unless designated at registration.
  • Attendees are able to check in at ANY time of the day
  • Make sure to present your Eventbrite registration in the form of a PRINTED TICKET or QR code from eventbrite on your phone!
  • ALL ATTENDEES MUST RSVP THROUGH EVENTBRITE [ HERE ]
  • All attendees will receive a NEON Orange wristband and one FREE raffle ticket at registration
  • Your wristband and passes serve as your entry into the venue, you are able to leave and return to the venue as you please as long as you have your wristband or passes!
    • ****NOTE: If you check in once on Saturday, you do NOT have to check in again for Sunday! Be sure to keep your wristband all weekend!****

FOR CSULB RESIDENTS

Make sure to have your STUDENT ID and your dorm location, the Housing Techs will be checking in you guys AFTER you receive your neon orange wristband at the front!

EQUIPMENT

  • This is a BYOC event - we encourage everyone to bring whatever game and setup they'd like!
    • *** NOTE: The BYOC is on a FIRST COME, FIRST SERVE BASIS as we have LIMITED space!***
  • If you are bringing PC's (allowed for both free play and tournament), make sure to check in at the Equipment Desk. ALL PERSONAL EQUIPMENT MUST HAVE A LABEL!
